About Us

Founded for teams that need reliable sensing in the real world.

We are incubated by National Institute of Technology Karnataka Science and Technology Entrepreneurship park (NITK-STEP) and the directors and founders have obtained their qualifications from reputed universities/institutes from India and abroad. We have got significant experience in the domain of radar systems, radar signal processing and target tracking . The consultants for this start-up are having a wide range of practical/ industry exposure in the area of radar system design.

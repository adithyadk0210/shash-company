About Us

Sri Shasha Prayathi Technologies Pvt. Ltd. was established on 19 February 2020 and is incubated at NITK-STEP, National Institute of Technology Karnataka. Founded and directed by Dr. Pathipati Srihari and Mr. Yeswanth Pattipati, the company develops advanced radar and intelligent sensing technologies for demanding real-world applications.

Our core focus includes short-range radar systems, RF transceivers, anti-drone radars, and high-speed signal-processing and target-tracking solutions. With expertise in radar system design, embedded FPGA platforms, real-time signal processing, and mmWave radar technology, we create reliable solutions for the defence, surveillance, and intelligent sensing sectors.

Our Capabilities

Technology Expertise

Strong Theoretical Background in Radar

Our team has a strong foundation in radar principles, electromagnetic systems, and target-detection techniques. This knowledge helps us transform complex concepts into accurate and dependable radar solutions.

Video, Radar Signal Processing and Tracking Algorithms

We develop real-time algorithms that process video and radar data to detect, classify, and track objects. Our solutions are designed to maintain reliable performance in complex and changing environments.

Expertise in Xilinx and Intel FPGAs

We implement high-speed processing architectures on Xilinx and Intel FPGA platforms for demanding applications. Our designs focus on low latency, efficient resource usage, and dependable system integration.

Expertise in All RF-Band Radars

Our capabilities cover FMCW and pulsed radar systems operating across multiple RF frequency bands. We select and develop suitable radar architectures based on range, resolution, and application requirements.

System Design and System Engineering

We manage the complete engineering cycle, from requirement analysis and architecture design to integration and validation. This structured approach ensures that every subsystem works together as a reliable solution.

Expertise in mmWave Radar Sensors

We build mmWave radar solutions for precise short-range sensing, motion detection, and object tracking. These compact systems support intelligent applications across defence, industrial, and civilian environments.

Advancing radar, RF, and intelligent sensing technologies for defence, surveillance, industrial, and academic applications.

Radar Waveform Generator

Key Features

  • Real-time generation of multiple radar waveforms
  • Support for LFM, Pulsed, Barker, Frank, and Polyphase-coded waveforms or any custom waveform
  • FPGA-based architecture for high-speed and deterministic operation
  • Programmable pulse width, PRI, bandwidth, and carrier frequency
  • Reconfigurable waveform parameters without hardware modifications
  • Multi-channel operation for phased-array and MIMO radar applications
  • Support for custom waveform development and rapid prototyping applications

Radar Target Echo Simulator

Key Features

  • Real-time generation of programmable radar target echoes
  • Independent control of target range, velocity, direction and RCS
  • Support for single and multiple target scenarios
  • Accurate Doppler shift and time-delay emulation
  • FPGA-based architecture for deterministic low-latency operation
  • High-fidelity digital RF memory (DRFM)-based target generation
  • Configurable clutter and interference simulation
  • Support for pulsed and FMCW radar systems
  • Multi-channel operation for MIMO and phased-array radars
  • User-configurable target profiles and motion trajectories
  • Hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) capability for radar testing

Electronic Defense Innovation Lab (EDIL)

The Electronic Defense Innovation Lab (EDIL) is a multidisciplinary platform focused on next-generation electronic warfare and radar system development. The lab integrates RF hardware, FPGA-based signal processing, and AI-driven analytics to enable hands-on learning and research in modern defense technologies.

It is designed to bridge the gap between theoretical concepts and deployable systems through real-time implementation.
